# Privacy Policy for SkyCast

**Last updated: March 13, 2026**

## Introduction

SkyCast ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use SkyCast.

### Location Information
With your permission, the App collects your device's location to provide weather information for your current area. Location data is sent to OpenWeatherMap's API servers to fetch weather data and is **not stored by us**.

### Automatically Collected Information
The App may collect certain information automatically, including:
- Device type and operating system version
- App usage data

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
SkyCast displays banner advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use data to show you relevant ads. This includes your Advertising ID, IP address, and general location. For more information, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### OpenWeatherMap API
Weather data is fetched from OpenWeatherMap. Your location coordinates (if permission is granted) are sent to their servers to fetch weather data. Please review [OpenWeatherMap's Privacy Policy](https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy).

## Data Storage

SkyCast stores weather data **locally on your device** using a local database for offline access. We do not upload this data to any server.

## Your Choices

- **Location Permission**: You can revoke location permission at any time in your device's Settings.
- **Ad Personalization**: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google Settings.

## Children's Privacy

SkyCast is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
